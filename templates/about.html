Our Purpose
🎯 The Mission

PyMarket was built as a Computer Science student project — a fully functional marketplace to buy and own high-quality Python source code. Not just a license, but true exclusive ownership. Every project is sold to exactly one buyer. Once it's yours, it's yours forever.

We believe in fair, transparent transactions via GCash 💙 — the payment method every Filipino knows and trusts. No complicated gateways, no hidden fees, just a simple QR code and you're done.
